HANKO is a Cyrillic font inspired by Japanese Kanji characters.

It is free for personal and commercial projects.

HANKO includes a capital letter design with full stop and comma for punctuation.

Based on the Serbian Cyrillic alphabet, the font has the following letters:
А, Б, В, Г, Д, Ђ, Е, Ж, З, И, Ј, К, Л, Љ, М, Н, Њ, О, П, Р, С, Т, Ћ, У, Ф, Х, Ц, Ч, Џ, Ш.


Instruction for the keyboard is included in the downloads.
